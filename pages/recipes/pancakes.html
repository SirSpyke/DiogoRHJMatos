Pancakes

In many homes, pancakes are a weekend specialty, as well as a more peaceful alternative to quick weekday breakfasts. They are also good for a snack or even as a main dish, without sugar and with a salty filling. Learn this recipe and try it with your family.

vegetarian

Easy | 30 min. | 15 servings

Ingredients

  • 150 g flour
  • 50 g sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 egg M
  • 200 ml semi-skimmed milk

Preparation

  • 1. In a bowl, mix the flour, sugar, yeast, salt, egg and milk. Mix it well with a whisk to get rid of any lumps.
  • 2. In a nonstick skillet and on a low heat, pour a tablespoon or more of the dough into the pan and let it spread.
  • 3. When the top of the pancake starts to make small bubbles, you can turn it over with the help of a spatula. Repeat the process until the dough is finished.
  • 4. Serve the freshly made pancakes with maple syrup, honey or even berries.
