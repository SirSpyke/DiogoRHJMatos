Red fruits smoothie with banana

For a brunch, snack or breakfast, this red fruit smoothie with banana is a real energy boost! Prepare in a food processor.

vegetarian

Easy | 2 min. | 4 cups

Ingredients

  • 300 g frozen red fruits
  • 1 banana
  • 20 g honey
  • 250 g semi-skimmed milk
  • as needed mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp instant oat powder

Preparation

  • 1. Grind the red fruits by pressing the Turbo button, three to four times, for 5 seconds.
  • 2. Scrape the sides of the glass with the spatula, add the banana cut into pieces, honey, milk, two or three mint leaves and the oat. Grind again for 40 seconds, gradually increasing the speed between medium and high.
  • 3. Pour into the cups.
